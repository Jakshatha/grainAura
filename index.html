What is GrainAura?

GrainAura is a solar-powered farming robot designed for paddy fields. It automates planting, watering, and weeding while being controlled through a mobile app. It's built for small and medium farmers in India looking to save time, water, and costs.

Farmers' Challenges

Many paddy farmers face high labor costs, unpredictable weather, and outdated tools. Manual work is exhausting and inefficient, especially in muddy fields. GrainAura solves these issues with technology tailored to their needs.

Key Features

1.Autonomous Operation

GrainAura runs on its own without manual control. It can plant, water, and weed automatically using sensors and smart navigation, saving farmers time and effort.

2.Smart Irrigation

Using real-time soil and weather data, the robot delivers water only when needed. This reduces water waste and ensures healthier crops with efficient water use.

3.Eco-Frindly power

GrainAura is powered by solar energy, making it sustainable and cost-effective. It works even in off-grid areas without relying on fuel or electricity.
